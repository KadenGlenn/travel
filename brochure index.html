Sedona

Sedona is a beautiful city located in Arizona coated in gorgeous red dirt and orange rocks, which draws a hard contrast compared to the rest of Arizona. It is a bright colorful city with plenty of activities and very rich and full History behind it. And there are many reasons to move there, the point of this brochure is to show anyone who is interested in moving to Arizona. Firstly, Arizona is located in the southwest part of The United States of America, Arizona as a whole is very diverse ranging from very rural parts with dust, nearly no trees, and rattlesnakes everywhere, to very beautiful cities like Flagstaff or Sedona, which have lush greenery and a variety of amazing animals and landmarks all around.
